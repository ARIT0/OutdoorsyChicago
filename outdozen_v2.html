How it works

The Rules

01

Once a Month, Every Month

Attend one approved outing each month for all 12 months of 2026. Miss a month and double up in any following month.

02

Qualifying Activities

Any OutDozen-branded activity counts. Camping trips also count.

03

The Goal: 24 for the Year

We're aiming for two OutDozen outings each month — a total of 24 experiences across all seasons. More chances to earn your spot.
