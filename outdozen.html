The OutDozen

12 Months · 12 Outings · 2026

The OutDozen exists to get people outside together — one unique outing a month, all year long. Whether it's a snowy hike, a camping trip under the stars, or a run through the cold, we believe the outdoors is better with your people.

12 Months
12 Required Outings
24 Goal Outings

The Mission

One outing a month.
All year long.

The OutDozen is a group outdoor challenge built for 2026. The vision is simple: get people outside together for unique outings all year long. Members commit to attending one approved outing per month for all 12 months — that's the dozen.

The goal is two outings per month, totaling 24 for the year. But the baseline is clear: one per month, every month, no excuses.

The Rules

What counts.

01

One qualifying outing per month is required to stay in the challenge. Miss a month, and you're out.

02

Any OutDozen-branded event automatically qualifies. These are planned, group-wide outings on the schedule.

03

Camping trips count — but they must be open to the group via the general chat and run at least dusk to dawn.

04

The goal is 24 outings (two per month). Hit the minimum of 12 and you complete the challenge. Hit 24 and you're a legend.
