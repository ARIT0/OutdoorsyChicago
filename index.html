Who We Are

Born in the city,
drawn to the wild.

We're a crew of Chicagoans — 20s and 30s somethings — who refuse to let city life be the whole story. Outdoorsy Chicago is where your neighborhood crew meets trail runners, kayakers, rooftop gardeners, and weekend wanderers.

Whether it's a sunrise at the Dunes, a midnight bonfire in the Northwoods, or finding a park you've never noticed in your own zip code — this is your community for all of it.
